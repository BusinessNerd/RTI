The Mission

Empowering the Next Generation of Musicians Through restoration and giving.

ReTune Idaho is dedicated to turning unplayed instruments into community value.

We reclaim forgotten instruments sitting in closets across the state, repair them to professional standards using local technicians, and gift them to students who have the drive but lack the equipment.

The Cycle of Equity

- 01

Reclaim

Instruments sitting in closets across Idaho, waiting for a new voice. We provide a tax-deductible path for donation.

- 02

Repair

Investing back into our community. We hire local Authorized Repair Partners to restore every instrument.

- 03

Resonate

A student is gifted the tool to master their craft, removing the financial barrier to musical excellence.
